Q-3 :- Canvas, Svg

Ans:-3 canvas and SVG have their strengths and weaknesses, and the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the project. Canvas is typically used for dynamic, pixel-based graphics, while SVG is more suitable for static or scalable vector graphics with the need for interactivity and accessibility.
